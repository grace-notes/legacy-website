The Ugly Indian (TUI) - Main Menu

The Ugly Indian is an anonymous group of people in Bangalore, India, who have developed a project for cleaning up unsightly places in their city. They have had marvellous success, and their vision is spreading to many other communities in India. You will really enjoy following their many activities, on YouTube and in Facebook photo albums.
