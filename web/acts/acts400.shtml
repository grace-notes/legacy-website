Acts 400 - chapters 22 to 28

compiled by Warren Doud.

Acts 400 is the fourth of four sections of the study of the Acts of the Apostles. Acts 400 covers chapters 22 through 28, and the topical and historical studies accompanying each lesson are shown in the table below.

The Acts 400 Grace Notes course has ten (10) lessons. The lessons are distributed by email. To register for the certificate course, write to Warren Doud.

The Topical and Historical studies can be downloaded from the Grace Notes Topics Library
