Christian Life

Text: The Christian Life and How to Live It, by W. H. Griffith Thomas.

William Henry Griffith Thomas (2 January 1861 – 2 June 1924) was an Anglican cleric and scholar from the English-Welsh border country. Griffith Thomas, with Lewis Sperry Chafer, was co-founder of Dallas Theological Seminary. See Wikipedia for biography: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Griffith_Thomas

From the preface:

The one theme running through the book is the Christian life, with its twofold attitude toward God and toward man, involving both the Lordship of Christ and the loyalty of Christians. "To this end Christ both died and lived again that He might be Lord" (Rom. 14:9); "Ye call Me Master and Lord: and ye say well; for so I am" (John 13:13) ; "Why call ye Me, Lord, Lord, and do not the things which I say?" (Luke 6:46).
