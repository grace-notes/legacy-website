One Year through the Bible

a Grace Notes Course

Bob Bolender, Pastor
Austin Bible Church
Austin, Texas

Pastor Bob taught this comprehensive Bible survey course in a series of 250 lessons over the period of one year. He has compiled a good synopsis of every chapter of the Bible! This course is an excellent introduction for any Christian, to provide a clear understanding of the Bible's organization and teaching. There is an audio file of Pastor Bob's class for each of the 250 lessons. You can listen to them by streaming audio (MP3) by using the links below.

There is also a Grace Notes course for the Through the Bible series. There are 20 lessons which cover the 53 weekly lectures. To register for this course, send email to: Warren Doud

Click on a link to view or download the PDF or Audio files.
