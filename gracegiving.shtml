Grace Notes Teacher Training Projects

All Grace Notes services or materials are free. All of the Authors of Grace Notes materials have graciously placed their work in the public domain, and they do not ask for compensation. The minimal overhead expenses are met by three or four regular donors who have supported the work for many years. No salaries are paid.

I hope that you will prayerfully consider adding resources to support the efforts of many pastor/teachers in Africa and other countries, who are using Grace Notes courses to train Bible teachers in their local churches.
