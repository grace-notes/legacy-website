The Epistle to the Ephesians

study compiled by Warren Doud

Ephesians is one of the most important books to study to learn what the plan of God is for the Christian's life. It is important to remember, that although we are saved by grace, we are called to live separated lives, lives of godliness. Sainthood is not the attainment of a select few, but it is the privilege and responsibility of every Christian. Eph. 2:10 states that we are "...created unto good works, which God has before ordained that we should walk in them."
