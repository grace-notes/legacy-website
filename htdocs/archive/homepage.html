Grace Notes Courses are available as studies leading to a certificate of completion. When you register, we will send you the first lesson.

When you have finished studying the lesson, complete the Quiz and return it to Grace Notes. Then, we will send you the next lesson.

When you have finished all lessons in a course, we will send you the course certificate.  When you have finished all of the courses in a Unit, we will send you an Achievement Award for completing that Unit.

To receive the Associate Bible Instructor diploma, you must complete Units I, II, III, and IV.

Go to the Registration Page to register. Or, view the course descriptions first.
